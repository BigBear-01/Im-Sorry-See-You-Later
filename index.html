— the truth —

ECA, I have been carrying this weight for a long time now, a weight made entirely of words I said that I did not mean, and feelings I buried because I thought they were better left unsaid. I was wrong. I owe you the truth.

That long, harsh message I sent you, the one where I said I never liked you the way you are, it was a lie. Every single word of it was a lie I told myself I had to tell you. Not because I felt it, but because I was afraid. Afraid that if I stayed, I would hold you back. Afraid that if I stayed, I would never fix the broken parts of me that I knew I needed to work on. I thought that you would be better without me, enjoy your life and do all the things You want.

So I chose the cruelest exit I could think of. I am so sorry that I use Your insecurities to express it, I thought if I pushed you away hard enough, You would move on easily, that it would hurt less than a quiet goodbye. But the moment I sent it, I felt something collapse inside me that I am still trying to piece back together.

✦

Here is what I should have said instead, You are beautiful, ECA. In every sense of the word. Not just the way you look, though the way you look still takes my breath away :>, but the way you treated me, the way you carry yourself, the quiet strength behind those text and voice, the warmth that radiates from you even in a still photograph. I never stopped seeing it. I never stopped feeling it.

You were, and still are, the only love of my life. Uncomparable. Unchanged. The one only. No version of me, not the broken one, not the one trying to grow, not any future version, will ever stop knowing that you were something rare and real and irreplaceable. You are the LOML, if I lost You then I have lost the meaning of "Love"

I am not writing this to ask you to come back. I am writing this because you deserve to know the truth, it was never about you. It was always, entirely, me, my fear, my shame, my selfishness in how I chose to go.

✦

I am so sorry, ECA. I am sorry for the words. I am sorry for the way I made you feel about yourself when the truth is that everything about you is worth loving. I hope you are kinder to yourself than I was. I hope you know, every single day, how genuinely beautiful and rare you are.

You teach me what love is, You gave me the motivation to live long enough so we can do all the things we said we would, That is what i have to reach now. To having You, as my life partner.

You asked me to delete your photos right? I would never. These photos are proof that beauty lives in every version of you, every mood, every light, every quiet moment you captured without realizing how much it would matter to someone. To me
